Building Information Modeling (BIM) is a digital process used in architecture, engineering, and construction industries.

BIM enables intelligent 3D modeling, coordination, clash detection, project visualization, quantity estimation, facility management, and lifecycle management.

LOD stands for Level of Development.

LOD 100 = Conceptual
LOD 200 = Approximate geometry
LOD 300 = Accurate geometry
LOD 400 = Fabrication level
LOD 500 = As-built verified model

Scan to BIM is the process of converting point cloud or LiDAR scan data into intelligent BIM models using software such as Autodesk Revit.
